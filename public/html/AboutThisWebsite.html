About this Website

Introduction:

The site is designed specifically for diabetics and helps them to manage their diabetes in the convenience of their own home, recording data that can be monitored remotely by their clinician. Our website currently consists of over 5,000 patients and over 100 clinicians who are located in Victoria, Australia. The site uses a variety of ways to encourage clinicians and patients to actively update their data. If you are a diabetic, come and join us. Adherence to treatment is the best way to change the situation.
